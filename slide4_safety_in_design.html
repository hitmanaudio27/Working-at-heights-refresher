SiD Principles

🎯

Early Intervention

Address height risks during design phase when changes are most cost-effective

🏗️

Multidisciplinary Approach

Engage designers, engineers, safety professionals, and constructors

⚡

Elimination Focus

Prioritise elimination of height work over protective measures

📋

Systematic Documentation

Record decisions and transfer remaining risks to construction

Elimination Examples
  • Prefabricate bridge segments at ground level
  • Design permanent guardrails during construction
  • Use precast elements to eliminate formwork
  • Plan utility installation before structure erection
  • Design access routes avoiding height work
  • Implement modular construction techniques
