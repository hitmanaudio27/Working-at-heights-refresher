EBTA Project Context

The Eastern Freeway Big Build represents one of Victoria's largest infrastructure projects, with extensive height work across bridge construction, overhead gantries, and elevated roadways.

Project Scale Impact

With thousands of workers across multiple construction phases, implementing EBTA's prevention-first approach is critical to achieving zero harm outcomes on this major government project.
